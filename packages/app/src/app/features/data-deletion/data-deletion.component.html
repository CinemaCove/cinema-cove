Data Deletion Request

How to delete your data

If you signed in to CinemaCove using Facebook and would like to have your data removed, please send an email to:

privacy@cinemacove.com

Include the following in your email:

  • The email address associated with your CinemaCove account
  • A brief note requesting data deletion

What gets deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account (email address, display name, Facebook ID)
  • All catalog configurations you have created
  • Any stored Trakt access tokens linked to your account

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email once complete.
