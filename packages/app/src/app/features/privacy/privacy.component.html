1. Who We Are

CinemaCove is a web application that allows users to create and manage custom Stremio addon catalogs powered by The Movie Database (TMDb) and Trakt.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use our service:

  • Email address — used to identify your account.
  • Display name — obtained from your Google or Facebook profile when you sign in with OAuth.
  • OAuth provider ID — a unique identifier from Google or Facebook, used to link your account.
  • Catalog configurations — the addon settings you create and save.
  • Trakt access tokens — if you connect your Trakt account, we store an access token to fetch your lists.

We do not collect passwords when you use OAuth login (Google/Facebook).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To store and serve your Stremio addon catalog configurations.
  • To fetch your Trakt watchlists, favorites, and ratings on your behalf.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

CinemaCove integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Facebook Login — for sign-in. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • The Movie Database (TMDb) — for movie and TV show metadata.
  • Trakt — for personal watchlists and ratings, if you choose to connect your account.
  • Google AdSense — we display ads on this site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits. See Google's ad policy.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session and to serve personalised ads via Google AdSense. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or via the consent banner shown on your first visit.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).
